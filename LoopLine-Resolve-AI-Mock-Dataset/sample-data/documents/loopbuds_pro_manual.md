# LoopBuds Pro Troubleshooting Manual · Synthetic Edition

## Product identifiers

Model: LBP-2. Serial format: LBP2-###. Charging case part: LBP2-CASE-01.

## Reset

Place both earbuds in the case. Hold the rear button for fifteen seconds until the indicator alternates blue and white.

## Intermittent charging

Clean dry charging contacts with a soft cloth. Do not use liquid. If the indicator remains off, test a supported charging cable and request proof of purchase.

## Visual evidence

An undamaged exterior does not prove that the battery, case contacts, or charging circuit is functional.
